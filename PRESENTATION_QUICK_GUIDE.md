## ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Q1: "How is this different from Apple Watch?"

**Answer:**
- "Apple Watch measures body vitals (heart rate, steps) but **cannot measure posture or sitting behavior**"
- "Our chair measures **pressure distribution, spine alignment, and micro-movements** - things a watch can't detect"
- "More importantly, our chair **physically intervenes** - it vibrates, changes LED color"
- "We also integrate **workstation context** - knowing when you're in deep work vs idle"

### Q2: "What's the patent angle?"

**Answer:**
- "The **multi-modal sensor fusion algorithm** - combining pressure, IMU, and environment sensors into a unified score"
- "The **adaptive intervention policy** - not just fixed reminders, but context-aware feedback"
- "The **privacy-preserving work context integration** - enhancing assessment without content monitoring"
- "The **auto-calibration method** - adapting to individual body types automatically"
- "The **sedentary risk index calculation** - specific algorithm for quantifying risk"

### Q3: "Real-world application?"

**Answer:**
- "**B2B**: IT companies, call centers - reduce employee health issues, improve productivity"
- "**B2C**: Home office workers, students - prevent chronic pain"
- "**Healthcare**: Rehabilitation centers, ergonomic clinics - track patient progress"
- "**Research**: Ergonomic studies, productivity research"

### Q4: "Cost and scalability?"

**Answer:**
- "Prototype cost: ₹3,200-4,000"
- "Bulk production: Can reduce to ₹2,000-2,500 per unit"
- "Scalable: Cloud-based dashboard, multiple chairs per organization"
- "ROI: Prevents costly health issues, reduces sick days"

### Q5: "What if sensors break?"

**Answer:**
- "Sensors are modular and replaceable"
- "System degrades gracefully - if one sensor fails, others continue working"
- "Easy maintenance - sensors can be replaced individually"

### Q6: "Privacy concerns?"

**Answer:**
- "No cameras, no content monitoring"
- "Only activity patterns (typing duration, idle time)"
- "All data stored locally or encrypted in cloud"
- "Privacy-preserving by design"

### Q7: "Accuracy?"

**Answer:**
- "Auto-calibration adapts to individual users"
- "2-minute calibration period establishes baseline"
- "Accuracy improves with usage"
- "Multi-sensor fusion increases reliability"
