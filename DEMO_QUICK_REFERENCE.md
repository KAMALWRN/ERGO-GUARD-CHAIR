# ErgoGuard Chair - Demo Quick Reference Card

## 🎯 Key Points to Emphasize

### 1. Problem Statement
- **"IT workers sit 8-12 hours daily, leading to chronic back/neck pain"**
- **"Existing solutions (Apple Watch) only measure vitals, not posture"**
- **"Our chair measures what wearables can't: posture, weight distribution, sitting behavior"**

### 2. Unique Value Proposition
- ✅ **Posture Detection** - Pressure sensors measure spine alignment
- ✅ **Sedentary Risk** - Micro-movement analysis over hours
- ✅ **Physical Intervention** - Chair responds (vibration, LED)
- ✅ **Privacy-Preserving** - No cameras, no content monitoring
- ✅ **Auto-Calibration** - Adapts to individual body types

### 3. Patent Potential
- **Multi-modal sensor fusion** (pressure + IMU + environment)
- **Adaptive intervention policy** (context-aware, not fixed intervals)
- **Privacy-preserving work context integration**

## 🎬 5-Minute Demo Flow

### Setup (30 seconds)
1. Show chair with sensors installed (hidden, LED visible)
2. Open dashboard on laptop/tablet
3. Point out: "All sensors embedded, no external devices needed"

### Demo 1: Good Posture (30 seconds)
- **Action**: Sit correctly
- **Show**: Dashboard → Green score (85+), LED green
- **Say**: "When posture is good, score stays high, LED is green"

### Demo 2: Posture Deviation (30 seconds)
- **Action**: Slouch forward
- **Show**: Score drops to 60, LED turns amber, vibration activates
- **Say**: "System detects forward lean, provides immediate feedback"

### Demo 3: Asymmetry (30 seconds)
- **Action**: Lean to one side
- **Show**: Dashboard shows weight distribution graph, asymmetry alert
- **Say**: "Detects uneven weight distribution, prevents long-term issues"

### Demo 4: Sedentary Risk (30 seconds)
- **Action**: Sit still for 30 seconds
- **Show**: Micro-movement drops, score decreases, break suggestion
- **Say**: "Monitors movement patterns, suggests breaks before fatigue sets in"

### Demo 5: Analytics (2 minutes)
- **Show**: Daily trend graph, posture breakdown, intervention history
- **Say**: "Long-term tracking helps identify patterns and improve habits"

## ⚠️ Common Questions & Answers

### Q: "What if sensors break?"
**A**: "Sensors are modular and replaceable. System degrades gracefully - if one sensor fails, others continue working."

### Q: "Privacy concerns?"
**A**: "No cameras, no content monitoring. Only activity patterns (typing duration, idle time). All data stored locally or encrypted in cloud."

### Q: "Battery life?"
**A**: "Powered via USB adapter (wall socket). No battery needed. Can add battery backup for portability."

### Q: "Comfort?"
**A**: "Sensors are thin and flexible. Users report no discomfort. Can be integrated into any chair design."

### Q: "Accuracy?"
**A**: "Auto-calibration adapts to individual users. 2-minute calibration period establishes baseline. Accuracy improves with usage."
